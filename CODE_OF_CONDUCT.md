# BOSC Community Library - Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The BOSC Community Library is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for all contributors and community members, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Demonstrating patience and understanding with newcomers

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech based on protected characteristics
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Deliberately disrupting community discussions
- Spam or promotional content
- Any conduct that violates applicable laws

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, including:
- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Issue and pull request comments
- Community forums and chat channels
- In-person events organized by the community
- Official project communications

## Reporting and Resolution

Instances of abusive, harassing, or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at [maintainers@bosc-library.org]. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent consequences as determined by other members of the project leadership.
